WELCOME GUARDIAN !

Your role is protecting the history from evil dragon who want to change the flow of history and destroy the present. During the storm dragon was lucky and he stole three pages from the book which stored the history of the inventions which changed the history. If you would not return them, the history would be change forever. In order to return those pages you need to play a simple game. The name of the game is "Elements". You have four elements of the nature in your possetion, and the dragon has the same set of elements. Those elements are: earth, wind, water and fire. You need to choose and element, and dragon will choose the element. Those elements would fight and would either loose or win. The winner would be the one who would gain 5 winning points. In order to win a game you need to win all three game to get all the pages back. After each game you would be asked if you want to contimue. Here the rules of how the winning points could be gained: Fire and Wind are working together, so if you have this combination both of you will gain a point. Water and Fire, water will put dow the fire, and one point would added to the player who choose water. Earth and Water, earth would make a water dirty, one point would be added to the player who choose earth. Wind and Water, wind will spread around water and make it weak, one point would be added to player who choose wind. Fire and Earth, fire would dry out earth, one point will be added to player who choose fire. In all other cases points would not be added.
